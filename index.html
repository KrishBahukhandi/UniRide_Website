About the app

UniRide makes everyday campus travel simpler, more coordinated, and more reliable.

UniRide is a ride-sharing app designed for campus and community transportation. It gives verified users one place to offer rides, request seats, review ride participation, and manage upcoming or completed trips without scattered chats or manual coordination.

The app solves a common problem for students and local commuters: arranging trusted rides quickly. Instead of relying on fragmented group messages, users get a dedicated workflow for ride discovery, approval, trip status, communication, and notifications.

Features

Everything users need to coordinate rides inside the app.

UniRide is structured around real ride-sharing actions, from setup and matching to approval, communication, and trip history.

Offer a ride

Drivers can create ride listings with route, timing, seat availability, and trip details for other verified users to view.

Request a ride

Riders can browse relevant trips, request to join, and see ride details before submitting a seat request.

Ride approval flow

Drivers can approve or decline ride requests so participation is intentional, visible, and easy to manage.

My Rides management

Users can review upcoming and past rides, monitor statuses, and keep track of ride activity over time.

In-app notifications

Ride-related notifications keep users updated on requests, approvals, reminders, status changes, and important trip events.

Ride chat

In-app messaging helps riders and drivers coordinate around specific rides without using external tools.

Profile and vehicle setup

Users can manage profile details, contact information, and driver vehicle information where applicable.

Safety, trust, and community

University email verification and ride participation controls help support a more trusted campus sharing experience.

FAQ

Common support questions.

I can’t verify my university email. What should I do?

Make sure you used your valid university email address and check your inbox or spam folder for the verification message. If the email still does not arrive, contact support with your university domain and the address you tried to use.

How do ride approvals work?

When a rider requests a seat, the driver can review the request and approve or decline it inside the app. Approved riders should receive updated ride details and relevant notifications.

Why does UniRide request location access?

Location may be used for ride matching, route display, pickup and drop-off coordination, and other trip-related features. If a user disables location permissions, some parts of the app may not work fully.

How can I request account or data deletion?

Users can contact privacy@uniride.app to request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data, subject to applicable law and verification requirements.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information needed to operate ride-sharing functionality, and certain technical information to keep UniRide secure and reliable.

Information you provide

  • Your name.
  • Your email address, including university email for verification.
  • Your phone number, if collected for ride coordination, account verification, or support.
  • Your profile details, such as profile photo, university affiliation, preferences, or account information.
  • Vehicle details you add if you offer rides, such as make, model, color, or similar ride-related driver information.

Information collected through app use

  • Location data when used for ride matching, route display, pickup and drop-off coordination, or trip-related features.
  • Ride and booking or request data, including offered rides, requested rides, approvals, declines, ride status, schedules, and ride history.
  • Messages or chat data where ride-related in-app messaging is available.
  • Notification and communication data related to ride reminders, request updates, approvals, or status changes.
  • Device, log, crash, and app usage data, such as device type, operating system, app version, IP address, and general diagnostics or analytics information if used.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create accounts, support login, and complete university or account verification.
  • To operate core ride functionality, including posting rides, requesting seats, approving or declining requests, and managing trip participation.
  • To support ride notifications, status updates, and in-app communication between riders and drivers.
  • To maintain profile and vehicle information where relevant to app use.
  • To support safety, respond to support requests, investigate misuse, and maintain service integrity.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and product quality using analytics or diagnostics where applicable.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only as needed to operate UniRide, meet legal obligations, or support the services users request.

  • With secure backend or cloud providers used for authentication, database hosting, storage, or app infrastructure.
  • With maps, routing, or location providers if UniRide uses those services for trip-related features.
  • With notification, messaging, crash reporting, or analytics providers where such tools are used to run or improve the app.
  • With law enforcement, regulators, or other parties when required by law or when necessary to protect rights, safety, or property.
